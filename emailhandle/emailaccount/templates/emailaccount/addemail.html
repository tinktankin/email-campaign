How to create an app password

Please Allow Us to use your Gmail for sending and receiving mail without giving your Gmail Password. This will be achieved by creating the application password for our app. Learn More.

Steps to create the app password

1. Go to account setting with your account. ( Click Here For Google Click Here for Outlook)
2. In left tabs, choose Security.
3. Under Signing in to Google, Choose App Password.
4. Confirm your identity.
5. Choose Custom.
6. Write TinkComm as the name and Generate the Password.
7. Copy the password and paste the text below.
8. Submit!
